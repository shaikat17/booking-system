    A Few words about us

    Academylia An overseas study consultancy by university teachers

    Introduction

    Higher education abroad provides an exceptionally incredible atmosphere to compare insights and to develop philosophy for individuals. It provides an opportunity for individuals to train themselves in a global environment. That is why; they are called foreign-trained individuals. A good number of students travel abroad from Bangladesh each year to fulfill their dream. Some feel confident choosing their pathways, some do not. Academylia wishes to contribute for those who need assistance to design the overseas study journey. The Greek name ‘Lia’ means ‘bearer of good news’. Academylia wants to convey good news by providing pathways to students intending for higher education abroad.

    Who we are?

    Academylia is a network of teachers from different universities of Bangladesh. The network provides free counseling and admissions services to the students intended for study in the UK. Most of the members of the network are foreign-trained. Some are in the pipeline for higher education abroad. That makes the network well-informed about current know-how globally. That is how Academylia stands out from other consultancies. The network provides services without any charge and avoids any direct financial transaction with students. The network promises to maintain the ethical standard and professional commitment.

    What we do?

    Academylia provides-

    1. one-to-one counseling service
    2. guidance to choose the program and university
    3. support to meetup the admissions requirements
    4. support to meetup English requirements
    5. support to write up a good SOP and CV
    6. support for admission application
    7. support to submit the visa application
    8. guidance for international career opportunities
